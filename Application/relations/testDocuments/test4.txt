Yet, as we venture further into the cosmos, we're also reminded of the fragility of our own planet.
Earth is the cradle of all known life, a rare oasis in an otherwise hostile universe.
The perspective gained from space — seeing our world as a tiny, delicate sphere suspended in darkness — has inspired a deeper sense of stewardship and unity.
As we dream of reaching new worlds, we carry with us not only the ambition to expand, but the responsibility to protect the one home we know for certain we have.
